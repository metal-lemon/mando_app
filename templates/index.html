Mandarin Learner

Track characters you know and get suggestions for words you can learn because you already know all their characters. Build your vocabulary systematically.

Can I Read This?

Paste any Chinese text, upload your character list, and see the percentage you already know. Click on unknown characters to mark them known and export an updated list.

Study Guide Generator

Turn unknown characters (exported from Can I Read This?) into a personalized study guide. Includes example sentences from your text and compound words using your known characters.

📖

Story Suggester

Find stories you're ready to read based on characters you know. Stories are ranked by difficulty, with both occurrence and unique character percentages. Long texts can be split into chunks.

Story Library

Manage your story database – view, add, edit, delete, and export stories. Build your personal collection of reading materials.

Traditional Finder

Identify traditional characters in any text. Load your own list or use the built-in list. Perfect for recognizing characters that differ from simplified Chinese.

Curriculum Unifier

Unified tool combining all curriculum builders. Select source, algorithm, and mode. Build paths, generate pools, or find clusters.

All tools work offline and store your data locally in your browser. Export your progress to share or back up.

Recommended workflow: Mandarin Learner → Can I Read This? → Study Guide Generator → Story Suggester → learn → repeat. Use Story Library to manage your story collection.
